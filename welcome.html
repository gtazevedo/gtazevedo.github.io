Welcome

The main idea of this website is to share some of my knowlege about financial risk and programming to try helping those seeking some information about these topics. When I entered this world, I had quite a difficult time trying to find information about these topics, so I want to make it a little easier to those who find this page, though I may lack content.

I'll hardly go into deep details in any topics but I'll always leave links or books with more details, so you know at least some places you can look into. Everything I've shared are my annotations or implementations of something I studied or am studying. If anyone wants to contribute, feel free to do so, just contact me.

A little bit about myself

I won't go into long boring details, but I think if you came here you want to know a little bit about me to check if you can trust at least a little bit in the things I'm writing. Well, I do hope so, but I'll tell a little bit of myself to see if I can gain a little bit of your confidence.
